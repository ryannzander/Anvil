# Code of Conduct

Anvil is a small project, but we want it to be a pleasant place to work.

## In short

- Be respectful. Critique code, not people.
- Assume good faith. Most disagreements come from missing context.
- Keep technical discussion technical. Take heated arguments offline.
- No harassment of any kind. Project maintainers will remove participants
  who make the space unwelcoming for others.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, contact a
maintainer listed in [`MAINTAINERS`](MAINTAINERS). Reports will be handled
discreetly.
